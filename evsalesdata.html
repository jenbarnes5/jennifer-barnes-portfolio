Transforming Raw Data into Market Strategy: An EV Sales Analysis

This project showcases the process of converting raw EV sales data into meaningful market strategies. While this analysis is based on publicly available sample data, it demonstrates fundamental techniques used in real-world business intelligence.

Key Insights

1. Target High-Performing Regions

EV sales are strongest in Oceania, Africa, and North America. Marketing and sales efforts should prioritize these high impact regions. Consider developing local incentives along with infrastructure improvements.

2. Brand-Specific Strategy

With Kia, BMW, and Ford leading sales, partnering with these manufacturers will continue to enhance sales. Consider special promotions, trade-in programs, and customer retention strategies.

3. Focus on Popular Vehicle Types

The hatchback category dominates EV sales, it's recommended that focus be placed on inventory and advertising tailored to this segment. SUVs and trucks also show strong performance, making them secondary priorities.

4. Assess Customer Segmentation

The budget-conscious Customer represents out paces the others. Improve sales through offering cost-effective financing, rebates, and lease options. Also consider sustainability incentives that will appeal to eco-conscious and high-income customers alike.
