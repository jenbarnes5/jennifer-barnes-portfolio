Transforming Complex Data into a Clear Strategy

This project showcases the process of converting electric vehicle sales data into meaningful market strategies. While this analysis is based on publicly available sample data, it demonstrates fundamental techniques used in real-world business intelligence.

Key Insights

1. Target High-Performing Regions

Electric vehicle sales are strongest in Oceania, Africa, and North America. Marketing and sales efforts that prioritize these high impact regions will drive continued growth. Establish localized incentives that align with regional infrastructure investments such as expansion of charging capabilitue and delivery of key improvements.

2. Brand-Specific Strategy

With Kia, BMW, and Ford leading sales, partnering with these manufacturers will continue to enhance sales. Consider special promotions, trade-in programs, and customer retention strategies.

3. Focus on Popular Vehicle Types

The hatchback category dominates EV sales, focus on inventory and advertising tailored to this segment. SUVs and trucks follow with strong performances, consider applying compareable marketing techniques to these groups as priority #2.

4. Understanding Customer Segments

Budget-conscious customers outpace the other segments when it comes down to purchasing power. Increase sales by offering cost-effective financing along with discounts, rebates and competitive lease options. Additionally, sustainability incentives will attract both eco-conscious and high-income buyers.
